Welcome to BasmaPep Database

BasmaPep-The Basmati rice peptide database is developed using experimentally derived peptides from the mass spectrometry analysis of Indian Basmati rice seed proteome. The peptides with more than 7 amino acids are presented in the database. It has 164373 number of peptides the protein identification score and Modification of 0 to 4255.809p. The search page allows for complex querying of peptides based on accession number, peptide length, peptide sequence, score, and modifications. BasmaPep database has inbuilt BLAST tool, MAP tool, PepCalc for identifying, mapping and analysing the physiochemical properties of the query peptide sequence. The enabled link to NCBI-BLAST will help researchers to locate their query sequence with the global datasets. The inbuilt link to Anti-microbial peptide database is another attraction for researchers. Apart from supporting the computational analysis, the login and data submission pages of BasmaPep ensures secure access and allow registered users to contribute new peptide data. This integrated approach allows researchers to explore, analyse, and share rice peptide data, supporting advancements in crop research and bioinformatics.
