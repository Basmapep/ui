Tools

Several bioinformatics tools are provided either as a web-service or standalone software to support your specific needs.

Blast Tool

Blast your target sequence against BasmaPepDB or NCBI database.

Blast BasmaPepDB NCBI BLAST

Map Tool

Map your target sequence against BasmaPepDB or NCBI nt database.

PepCalc Tool

Analyze your Peptide properties target sequence against BasmaPepDB.

APD3

Antimicrobial Peptide Calculator and Predictor

Analyze your Peptide properties against Antimicrobial PeptideDatabase (AMP).

CAMPR4: Predict AMP sequences

Predict your Peptide properties using CAMPR4 Database with RF,SVM and ANN.
